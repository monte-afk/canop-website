How it works

You do two things.
We do the rest.

Step 01
Submit a brief
  • Location, size, duration
  • Any specific requirements
  • Takes two minutes
Step 02

  • By next business day
  • Vetted for host track record, condition & amenities
  • Some inspected in person
Step 03
Choose and confirm
  • Select the property
  • We handle payments & agreements
  • Keys ready on arrival date
Step 04
One contact throughout
  • Questions, changes, disputes — us
  • Hand back the keys at checkout
  • We sort everything from there
